# SRIntelligence — Product Roadmap

Status key: `[ ]` planned · `[x]` shipped · `[-]` idea

---

## Core Platform

- [ ] **Workflow Versioning UI** — View diffs between workflow versions directly in the timeline hover menu. *(High)*
- [ ] **Schedule Wiring** — Connect the schedule picker to a real cron API with timezone support. *(High)*

---

## AI Depth & Reasoning

- [ ] **AI Workflow Builder** — Describe a workflow in plain English and have the AI generate the node graph automatically. *(High)*
- [ ] **Explainable AI + Confidence Scores** — Every result shows the reasoning chain, key assumptions, and a confidence score with plain-English caveats. *(High)*
- [ ] **Hypothesis Testing Mode** — State a hypothesis in plain English; the system runs causal analysis, controls for confounders, and returns a structured verdict. *(High value)*
- [ ] **Autonomous Insight Discovery** — Scheduled background job scans key metrics weekly and surfaces anomalies unprompted — before you ask. *(Differentiator)*
- [ ] **Competitive Intelligence & Market Research Triangulation** — Ingest CI reports and market research publications to triangulate insights against internal analytics — automatically surfacing where external sources confirm, contradict, or extend your internal findings. *(Differentiator)*

---

## Presentation & Sharing

- [ ] **Shared Dashboard** — Compose multiple workflow result artifacts into a pinned, auto-refreshing dashboard grid. *(High)*
- [x] **Story Mode / Narrative Reports** — One click converts a workflow run into an AI-written executive report. Export to PDF or PowerPoint. *(Must-have)*
- [ ] **Embeddable View-Only Links** — Share any result via a time-limited, read-only URL — no login required for external stakeholders. *(High)*
- [ ] **Annotation & Commentary Layer** — Stakeholders leave comment threads on charts or workflow nodes, keeping insights and context together. *(High value)*

---

## Enterprise & Compliance

- [ ] **HIPAA Audit Trail & Data Lineage** — Immutable log of who queried what, when. Visual lineage graph from source tables to every result. *(Must-have)*
- [ ] **Territory & Rep Access Controls** — Row-level security tying user identity to territory/district/region — field teams only see their geography. *(Must-have)*
- [ ] **Approval Workflows for Reports** — Require a reviewer sign-off before a forecast or analysis reaches leadership. Compliance-grade chain of custody. *(High value)*

---

## Collaboration

- [ ] **Real-Time Multiplayer Canvas** — Multiple users editing the same workflow simultaneously with live presence cursors — like Figma for analytics. *(High)*
- [ ] **@Mention & Task Assignment** — Tag a colleague on a finding; creates a lightweight task loop without leaving the platform. *(High value)*

---

## Proactive Intelligence

- [ ] **Natural Language Alerts** — "Alert me when Brand X share drops below 15%" — set in plain English, delivered in-app and via email. *(High)*
- [ ] **Forecast Deviation Monitoring** — Automatically compares actuals against prior forecasts and flags meaningful deviations on every run. *(High value)*

---

## Integrations

- [ ] **Veeva CRM & IQVIA Connectors** — Pull call activity, targeting data, and syndicated market data natively — where pharma data already lives. *(High)*
- [ ] **Slack / Teams Push** — When a workflow completes or an alert fires, push the summary into a Slack channel or Teams chat. *(High value)*
- [ ] **Reverse ETL — Push Results to Snowflake** — Write workflow outputs (segments, forecasts, scores) back as Snowflake tables for downstream systems. *(Differentiator)*

---

## Accessibility & Field

- [ ] **Mobile-Responsive Field Dashboards** — Stripped-down mobile view for field reps: territory Rx trends, target performance, alerts — on a phone. *(High value)*
- [x] **Voice-to-Query** — Microphone button in chat input — speak a question and it's transcribed, routed, and executed. *(Differentiator)*

---

## Benchmarking

- [ ] **Anonymized Peer Benchmarking** — Opt-in anonymized comparison across the customer base. A network-effect moat no individual vendor can replicate. *(Differentiator)*
